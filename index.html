About Me

Hello! I'm a passionate UI/UX and graphics designer with over 1 years of experience creating digital experiences that matter. My journey began with a fascination for how design can bridge the gap between technology and human needs.


I specialize in user-centered design, bringing together research, strategy, and creative thinking to solve complex problems. Whether it's designing intuitive interfaces, creating compelling visual identities, or optimizing user journeys, I'm driven by the impact design can have on people's lives.


When I'm not designing, you'll find me exploring new design tools, reading about psychology, or sketching ideas in my notebook. I believe the best designs come from understanding both technology and human behavior.
